Lee Felix and Ambassadorships

Louis Vuitton

Felix of Stray Kids has emerged as a prominent figure in the fashion world through his collaboration with Louis Vuitton. On August 22, 2023, he was appointed as a house ambassador for the brand, with artistic director Nicolas Ghesquière praising his “audacious sense of style” . Felix made his runway debut at Paris Fashion Week on March 5, 2024, walking for Louis Vuitton’s Women’s Fall-Winter 2024 Collection. He described this experience as one of the “happiest moments” of his life . He returned to the runway on March 10, 2025, for the brand’s Fall-Winter 2025 show, showcasing his evolving presence in high fashion. Beyond the runway, Felix has been featured in several fashion editorials, including the May 2025 issue of Esquire Korea, where he modeled Louis Vuitton pieces . His appearances at major events, such as the 2025 Met Gala and Louis Vuitton’s Cruise 2025 show in Barcelona, further solidify his status as a fashion icon. Felix’s partnership with Louis Vuitton exemplifies the synergy between K-pop and luxury fashion, highlighting his influence as both a musician and a style ambassador.

Samsung

In May 2025, Felix of Stray Kids was announced as the new face of Samsung’s Galaxy S25 Edge, the company’s slimmest smartphone to date. The device, measuring just 5.8mm in thickness and weighing 163 grams, boasts a 200-megapixel wide-angle camera and integrates Samsung’s AI suite, Galaxy AI. Felix’s collaboration with Samsung was unveiled through a cinematic Instagram reel, where he showcased the phone’s sleek design while dressed in a sophisticated black suit. The campaign, under the slogan “Beyond Slim,” highlighted both the phone’s design and Felix’s modern aesthetic. Samsung teased the partnership by releasing a silhouette of Felix’s face, prompting fans to guess his identity, with Starbucks gift cards offered as prizes for correct guesses. This collaboration continues Samsung’s tradition of partnering with K-pop stars, following previous campaigns featuring BTS and BLACKPINK. Felix’s involvement has generated significant buzz among fans, with many expressing excitement over the partnership. The Galaxy S25 Edge is set to go on sale in the U.S. on May 30, 2025. Felix’s dual roles as a fashion icon and tech ambassador underscore his growing influence in both industries. His collaboration with Samsung exemplifies the synergy between K-pop and global brands.
