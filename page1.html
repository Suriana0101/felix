Felix's family and childhood

Family

Lee Felix was born in Catholic family of Korean Immigrants in Sydney, Australia. His family consists of his mother, father, an older sister named Rachael(Korean name Jisue) and a younger sister named Olivia(Korean name Jimin). His parents are both of Korean Descent, but were born in South Korea and later emigrated to Australia. His family is known to be close and supportive of one another. Felix was raised in an environment centered on kindness, humility, and gratitude. Felix's mom is involved in interior design, architecture, properly styling, visual merchandising, floristry, fashion design, photography and food styling. Little is known about Felix's older sister and dad, as they don't publish anything in social medias. However, his younger sibling Olivia has TikTok and Insta and shares more about her life.

Childhood

Felix grew up in a vibrant and multicultural environment. He was attending a Catholic School that emphasized faith and moral values. Felix spent much of his childhood practicing taekwondo, eventually earning a third-degree black belt and competing in tournaments. Music was also a big part of his upbringing; his mother encouraged him to learn the piano, which sparked his love for music and performing. Although shy a child, he showed a natural talent for dancing and singing, performing at school events. Felix enjoyed life in Sydney, often spending time outdoors and bonding with friends and family.
