Career

Stray Kids

Stray Kids is an 8-member K-pop group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017 through the survival show Stray Kids. The group officially debuted on March 25, 2018, with their album I AM NOT and the title track District 9. They are known for their self-producing approach, with members like Bang Chan, Han and Changbin (3RACHA) heavily involved in songwriting and production. The group's lineup includes:

  • Bang Chan
  • Lee Know
  • Changbin
  • Hyunjin
  • Han
  • Felix
  • Seungmin
  • I.N

Their music blends various genres like hip-hop, EDM, and Rock, creating a bold and experimental sound. Stray Kids gained global recognition with their hit song "God's Menu" in 2020, which showcased their signature energetic style. They've topped charts with albums such as ODDINARY and MAXIDENT, becoming one of the best-selling K-pop acts. Their live performances are known for their intensity and innovative choreography, drawing in fans worldwide. Stray Kids' fanbase, called STAYs, shares a deep connection with the group through their heartfelt and empowering music. The group's themes often explore self-discovery, resilience, and pushing boundaries, resonating with a global audience. Stray Kids continue to break records and redefine K-pop with their fearless creativity and unwavering passion.

Felix's role in Stray Kids

Lee Felix originally joined Stray Kids through the reality survival show Stray Kids, where he faced challenges, including a brief elimination before making a triumphant return to the final lineup. In the group, Felix serves as a dancer, vocalist, and one of the visual members. His deep, distinctive voice adds depth to their songs, while his charismatic stage presence captivates audiences worldwide. Felix is also known for his ability to connect with fans through his warm personality and genuine interactions.Expanding his artistic repertoire, Felix released his solo track “Unfair” in December 2024 as part of Stray Kids’ mixtape Hop. The song, inspired by the tale of Beauty and the Beast, delves into themes of inner struggle and the desire to be seen for one’s true self. Lyrics like “My life is so unfair, everyone sees me as if I’m the beast out there” reflect this sentiment. Felix’s performance in “Unfair” showcases his emotional depth and vocal versatility, earning praise for its heartfelt delivery. The track was featured in the group’s “dominATE” world tour, further highlighting his growth as a solo artist.
